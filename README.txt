== Description ==

<p>Speedy Video allows you to quickly and easily make a video from a WordPress page or post. You can upload that video to YouTube.</p>

<p>You can find more information on our web site (<a href="https://www.speedyvideo.net/">speedyvideo.net</a>).</p>

<p>Speedy Video works by taking your written text and by using a selection of computer generated voices and images, turns it into a video that can be posted to your website or YouTube.</p>

<p>Most people don't think of YouTube as a search engine, but the fact remains that YouTube is the second most popular search engine right behind Google. If you are not creating videos for your YouTube Channel, then you are ignoring an important mechanism to increase your SEO rankings and get more sales or leads.</p>

<p>Modern Search Engine Optimization relies more heavily on:</p>

<ul>
	<li><b>Social Media:</b> Google has been heavily weighting its ranking on Social Media for a while now.</li>
	<li><b>Fresh Website Content:</b> Blog posts or articles on a regular basis.</li>
	<li><b>YouTube Videos:</b> YouTube is the second biggest search engine on the planet. It's important to have an active presence on YouTube.</li>
	<li><b>Back Links:</b> As always, one way relavent, high quality back links to your website are super important.</li>
</ul>

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload `speedyvideo.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to the Speedy Video Section in your admin area.
